About Us

Parking Monitoing System (PMS) offers a variey of features and statistics for its user. It uses YOLOv5 detetcions with StrongSORT for object tracking and is deployed using Flask.

Live stream

We display the live stream on the website and also displaying the bounding boxes, confidence score and instance number with a short delay.

Parking Statistics

It shows a variety of statistics of parked cars such as the statistics for cars parked today, yesterday and whole week along with a dynamic graph and much more.

Security System

It extracts the boundig box of the car and displays its picture and number on the front end, if it moves past a specific threshold.
